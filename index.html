【31】The ginkgo tree is a very old tree. It was here when dinosaurs were on Earth. People call it a “living fossil.” The leaves are like small fans and turn bright yellow in fall. Many people like to walk under the yellow trees and take photos. The tree can live for a long time, even more than one thousand years. It is strong and can live in cities with smoke and cars. 　The ginkgo is very strong in fire and heat. In World War II, the atomic bomb fell on Hiroshima. The bomb made great heat and fire in the city. Many trees died in the fire, but some ginkgo trees lived and grew again. People saw hope in the new green leaves. This shows that the ginkgo is tough and can stand strong heat and fire. ja.wikipedia.org 　The ginkgo seed has a very strong smell. Some people do not like it. But the seed can be eaten after cooking. Many people enjoy it in food. In spring, flowers are small. In summer, leaves give cool shade. In fall, the leaves are bright yellow. In winter, the tree rests, and then the cycle starts again. 　In Japan, many schools and parks have ginkgo trees. Sanda Gakuen also has ginkgo trees, and students can see the yellow leaves in fall. The ginkgo is part of city life and school life. It is an old, strong, and beautiful tree that people can enjoy every year.
